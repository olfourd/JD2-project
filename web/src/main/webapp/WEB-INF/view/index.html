Reasons to play this game

Low system requirements

LoL does not require a steep computer to run. Ancient laptops, grandmother's computers, machines at work - minimal system requirements will allow running the game almost on the refrigerator!

Easy to learn

To fully adapt users, special Bots-mentors have been created, which help novice gamers to get used to the virtual world. In addition, each task is built in such a way as to gradually train you.

Regular updates and support for developers

LoL is the only project Riot is working on. Therefore, developers pay special attention to it. It is regularly updated and updated.

friendly community

Compared with other games in the genre MOBA, you will meet a lot less inadequate players. Also in the game there is no voice communication and you do not have to listen to how to play from the twelve-year old pro players

Frequently Asked Questions


FREE CHAMPIONS OF THE WEEK

Every week 10 champions are selected, performing in different roles, for which you can play for free. This gives the callers the opportunity to try to play for the champions, who are interested in them. In addition, it increases the number of available champions for summoners who started playing not long ago.

WHAT RULES SHOULD BE AVOIDED WHEN CHOOSING THE NAME OF THE CALLER?

The names of all conscripts must comply with the Terms of Use. The most unseemly names and names that include profanity will be filtered automatically. But in the end, the task of choosing an acceptable name falls on you. The summoner's name must be at least 3 and not longer than 16 characters. The caller's name can not contain the word "Riot". Your caller name should not contain information that you can be identified. For example, your real name or address. If it turns out that your caller's name contains such information, Riot Games has the right to change it. An attempt to present yourself as a cyber sportsman or impersonate a cybersport is also considered a violation, even if the system allows you to choose a given name. Such names of summoners can also be changed.
